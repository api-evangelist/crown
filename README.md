# Crown Holdings (crown)

Crown Holdings, Inc. is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and sale of rigid packaging products for consumer goods, including metal beverage cans and ends, food cans, aerosol cans, specialty closures and promotional tins, as well as transit and protective packaging products for industrial markets. The company operates in more than 40 countries and supports customers with design, decoration, reprographics, compound development, pilot facilities, and seaming training services. Crown Holdings does not publish a public developer API program; technical collaboration with customers happens through B2B and EDI channels.
